Removing Birthday Notifications From Hotmail

  1. Click on the Settings cog wheel in the top right of the web page.
  2. Click on the "View all Outlook settings" link at the bottom of the settings window that appears.
  3. Click on the Calendar tab on the left.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and uncheck the "Turn on the birthday calendar" box.
