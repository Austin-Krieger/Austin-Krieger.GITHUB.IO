Installing

  1. Download the mod.
  2. Extract the folder.
  3. Drag the folder to your mods folder (D:\Steam\steamapps\common\PAYDAY 2).

Equipment Boost

This is my own personal mash up of mods. Want to hold hundreds of bags of near limitless equipment into the game with you? I got you. We also have: no fall damage, insane amounts of dodge chance, and much more. Secondary equipment counts will be halved. Some improvements require perk decks that increase health, armor, or stamina to take effect.

Carry Stacker

Pick up everything (Body bags, Gold, Paintings, Drills, etc.).

Download: CarryStackerReloaded.zip.

Silent Assassin

Silently take out guards without pagers going off. Headshots preferred.

Download: SilentAssassin.zip.

Better Bots

Improve friendly bots in various ways. Improved chance they will be able to pick you up when things go wrong at the beginning of a heist.

Download: BetterBots.zip.

No Detection

Download: NoDetection.zip.

No Drill Timers

Remove the timer for many drills. On the newest maps this will crash the game, but for the majority of mission you will be glad you have this. Some drills are hard coded to break a few times, just so you know.

Remove Timers
