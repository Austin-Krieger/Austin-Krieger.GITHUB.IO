Installing Mods (Non-Published)

  1. Download the mod.
  2. Extract the folder.
  3. Drag the folder to your mods folder (D:\Steam\steamapps\common\Don't Starve Together\mods).

Installing Mods (Steam)

  1. Open Don't Starve Together
  2. From the main menu click on Mods.
  3. Click on Get Mods and then click on More Mods to open the steam menu directly to the DST workshop.
  4. Subscribe to your desired mods.
