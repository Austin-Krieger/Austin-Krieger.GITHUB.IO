Soul Boxes

This tutorial adds soul boxes that give out rewards and/or open magic doors.

  1. Here are the folders you will need to open for this tutorial, starting with our root folder D:\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Duty Black Ops III\

    2. usermaps\zm_tutorial_level\sound\zoneconfig

    usermaps\zm_tutorial_level\scripts\zm

    share\raw\scripts\custom

    map_source\_prefabs\zm

  2. Download: SoulBoxes.zip
  3. Once you extract the files, open up the Soul Boxes folder and drag the contents into the root folder. You can also move the actual files into their appropriate folders yourself.
  4. Go to your Maps' zone_source folder and open up the zm_tutorial_level.zone file.
  5. Paste the following lines at the bottom of the file:

    6. // Soul Boxes
    scriptparsetree,scripts/custom/growing_soulbox.gsc
    fx,zombie/fx_ritual_pap_energy_trail

  6. Go to your Maps' scripts\zm folder and open the .gsc file. Paste the following lines underneath #using scripts\zm\zm_usermap;

    7. // Soul Boxes
    #using scripts\custom\growing_soulbox;

  7. Scroll down to the main() function and paste the following lines inside:

    8. // Soul Boxes
    grow_soul::init();

  8. Now we can open our map in Radiant.
  9. Right Click > Misc > prefab. Double Click the Soul_Boxes folder and open the grow_soul.map file.
  10. Once the soul box is filled in game, it will disappear and drop a reward.

Additional Reading

  1. If you add the grow_soul_door_rotate.map prefab to the map and fill the soul box with zombies in game, it will open the door when it is full. All the soul boxes need to be full to open the magic door, so you can have one or more soul boxes in your map.
