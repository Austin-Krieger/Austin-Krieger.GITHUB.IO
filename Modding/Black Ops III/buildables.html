Making a Buildable Power Switch

Want players to search for and be able to craft the hidden pieces of the power switch? Continue reading.

  1. Here are the folders you will need to open for this tutorial, starting with our root folder D:\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Duty Black Ops III\

    2. map_source\_prefabs

    model_export

    share\raw\sound\aliases

    usermaps\zm_tutorial_level\scripts\zm

    usermaps\zm_tutorial_level\sound\zoneconfig

  2. Download: Buildable_Power.zip
  3. Go to the map_source\_prefabs folder and make a Buildable_Power folder for our .map prefabs. You can also move the folders provided to your root folder and skip to step 6.
  4. Go to the model_export folder and paste the buildable_power_assets.gdt file.
  5. Now go to the share\raw folder and paste our custom_images folder.
  6. Next go to the share\raw\sound\aliases folder and open the .csv file of your choice. I went with the zmb_sound_fixes.csv file from a previous tutorial. We need to add the lines for the sound files we just added.

    7. build_loop,,,custom\buildables\build_loop.wav,,,UIN_MOD,,,,,,,,,,,99,100,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,
    build_done,,,custom\buildables\build_done.wav,,,UIN_MOD,,,,,,,,,,,99,100,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,
    part_pickup,,,custom\buildables\part_pickup.wav,,,UIN_MOD,,,,,,,,,,,99,100,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,,

  7. Open your Maps' .gsc file from the usermaps\zm_tutorial_level\scripts\zm folder.
  8. Inside the main() function add the following lines:

    9. // Buildable Power
    level thread custom_buildables_random::init();

  9. Scroll up to the line #using scripts\zm\zm_usermap; and paste the following lines after it:

    10. // Buildable Power
    #using scripts\zm\custom_buildables_random;

  10. Now we need to open our Maps' zone file zm_tutorial_level.zone from the zone_source folder and add the following lines at the end:

    11. // Buildable Power
    scriptparsetree,scripts/zm/custom_buildables_random.gsc
    xmodel,p7_zm_der_pswitch_body
    xmodel,p7_zm_der_pswitch_handle
    xmodel,p7_zm_zod_fuse
    material,power_panel_shader
    material,power_handle_shader
    material,power_fuse_shader

  11. Now it is time to open our map in Radiant.
  12. Inside your map Right Click > Misc > prefab.
  13. Double Click the Buildable_Power folder and open the master_power_switch.map file.
  14. Do the same for the power_fuse.map prefab and so on, until you have all the parts in your map. You can stamp said prefabs and rotate them however you like when placing them.
  15. With all the parts placed, you can make some copies and place those as well. Our script will choose which copy of each part is active when the map loads.
  16. You now have a buildable power switch.

Adding a Buildable Rocket Shield

Want players to search for and be able to craft the hidden pieces of the rocket shield? Continue reading.

  1. Here are the folders you will need to open for this tutorial, starting with our root folder D:\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Duty Black Ops III\

    2. map_source\_prefabs

  2. Download: Buildable_Rocket_Shield.zip
  3. Go to your map_source\_prefabs folder and create a folder called Buildable_Rocket_Shield and paste the assets inside or drag the provided folder into your root folder.
  4. Now we need to open our map in Radiant and place all of the .map files you just downloaded.
  5. Place 1 or more of each part just like we did with the Buildable Power tutorial and place as many fuel tanks for the shield as you want (zm_craftables_rocketshield_refill.map).
  6. One more important step is for the zm_craftables_rocketshield_spawner.map file. We need to place it under the floor of our map and make it roughly as large.
  7. Before you can resize it, we need to do Right Click > Prefab > Stamp Prefab.
