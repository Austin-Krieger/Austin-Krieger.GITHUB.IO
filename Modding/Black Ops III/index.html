Camera Movement

 
Action Control
Pivot Camera angle up toward the sky A
Pivot Camera angle down toward the ground Z
Move the camera straight up D (Tap to go up in increments, Hold to move faster)
Move the Camera straight down C (Tap to go up in increments, Hold to move faster)
Zoom in/out in larger increments Hold Alt + Use Scroll Wheel
Move Camera Forward Up Arrow ↑
Move Camera Backwards Down Arrow ↓
Rotate Camera left 15 degrees Left Arrow ←
Rotate Camera right 15 degrees Right Arrow →
Move Camera Up and Down in large increments Page Up / Page Down
Move Camera ←↓→↑ in larger increments Hold Right Click + Ctrl (Move mouse)
Move the map around in Grid View Hold Right Click in the Grid View and move
 

Changing Views

These are the shortcuts for changing what you can see in the Camera Pane & Grid Pane (XY Top, XZ Front, YZ Side).

Action Control
Flat View F7
Lighting View F8
Game View F9
Cycle Views in Grid Pane Ctrl + Tab

Duplicate Selected Item Space
Delete Items Backspace
Hide Selection H
Show the Last Items Hidden Ctrl + Shift + H
Show All Hidden Items Shift + h
Select Rotation Handles R
Toggle Edge Lines for Selection J
Cut (Removes Portion of the Selected Item) X, Select 2 Points in Grid View, Enter
Cut (Keep Both Parts as Separate Objects) X, Select 2 Points in Grid View, Shift + Enter

Making a Wall

  1. Search for caulk in the texture tab
  2. Left click in the Grid View to make the wall
  3. Click outside the walls area to stretch in that direction
  4. Click on the wall to move it, change grid view or do this in game view window
