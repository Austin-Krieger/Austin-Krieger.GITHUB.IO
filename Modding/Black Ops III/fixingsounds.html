Fixing Zombies Map Sounds

The mystery box, pack-a-punch machine, perk machines, and even some guns are missing sounds. This tutorial should fix that issue.

  1. Here are the folders you will need to open for this tutorial, starting with our root folder D:\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Duty Black Ops III\

    2. sound_assets

    share\raw\sound\aliases

    usermaps\zm_tutorial_level\sound\zoneconfig

  2. Download: SoundsFix.zip
  3. Once the file is downloaded, go to your sound_assets folder and move the downloaded sound assets.
  4. Next go to your aliases folder and move the zmb_sound_fixes.csv file from the share folder.
  5. Finally go to your Maps' sound > zoneconfig folder and open your .scz file.
  6. Add the new alias to the file:

    7.  {
     "Type" : "ALIAS",
     "Name" : "user_aliases",
     "Filename" : "user_aliases.csv",
     "Specs" : [ ]
     },
     {
     "Type" : "ALIAS",
     "Name" : "zmb_sound_fixes",
     "Filename" : "zmb_sound_fixes.csv",
     "Specs" : [ ]
    },

  7. As I mentioned in the changing weapon values tutorial, having certain attachments or too many attachments on a gun can prevent the firing sound for that gun from working.
