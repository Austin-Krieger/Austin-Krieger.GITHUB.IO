Uploading Mods to Steam Workshop

We will be uploading our simple Spawn Room Items mod to the Steam Workshop

  1. Here are the folders you will need to open for this tutorial, starting with our root folder D:\Steam\steamapps\common\The Binding of Isaac Rebirth\

    2. tools\ModUploader

    C:\Users\*****\Documents\My Games\Binding of Isaac Afterbirth+ Mods

  2. First thing you need to do is run the game with your mod enabled. This will create a metadata.xml file for the mod.


  3. Have a preview image for your mod prepared.
  4. Next you need to go to your tools\ModUploader folder and run ModUploader.exe
  5. Click Choose Mod... and select the newly created metadata.xml file
  6. Choose your tags. I went with Items and Rooms.
  7. Next you can add your preview image, choose the visibility, add change notes, and don't forget to add a description for your new mod.


  8. Hit Upload Mod and your mod will be available on the Steam Workshop. You now have an updated metadata.xml file with the choices you made in the uploader.
