Increased Difficulty - Seamless Co-op

Increase the damage, resistances, and health of the enemies based on your NG+ level starting at NG+0. This also comes with the option to make NG+0 = NG+7 (vanilla) difficulty.

Download: Increased Difficulty.zip.

Boss Resurrection + Grand Merchant

Resurrect Bosses using rune fragments and buy everything you want from merchant Kale. These can be installed seperately, but I included them in the same .zip file because they both use the menu.msgbnd.dcx file and merging text documents doesn't work the same as with .csv files.

Credit/Original:
Boss Resurrection: https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/2790
Grand Merchant: https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/129

Download: Boss Res + Grand Merchant.zip.

Dual Wield Grease + Perma Grease

Apply grease to both weapons and increase how long the grease will be applied to your weapons.

Credit/Original:
Grease Apply to Both Weapons: https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/1222

Download: Dual Wield Grease + Perma Grease.zip.

Spirit Summoning

Modify some of the spirit summons to summon other creatures or add to the list of summons such as the pack of lone wolves.

Credit/Original:
Summon Blaidd As a Spirit Ash: https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/1195
Melina as Spirit Summon: https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/284
Unlocked Spirit Ashes: https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/336

  1. Lone Wolf - Summon Blaidd along with the 3 wolves.
  2. Spirit Jellyfish - Summon Melina instead.
  3. Rotten Stray - Summon the Fire Giant instead.
  4. Nomad - Summon Lichdragon Fortissax instead.
  5. Spirits Unlocked - Less Summon Location Restrictions.

Download: Summoning.zip.

Credit/Original: Direwolf Mount https://www.nexusmods.com/eldenring/mods/2408
