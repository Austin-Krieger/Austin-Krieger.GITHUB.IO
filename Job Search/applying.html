Jan 26th - 28th 2018

That depends on the size of the company and what is being offered to the employees, sick leave can be earned at an average rate of 1 full sick day every 6 weeks or the employer could offer roughly 8 days for the year and another 10 for vacation days. How do you handle difficult or escalated customers?
When clients have issues, I use a calm tone of voice and educate them about our company’s services and policies. I won't take their anger personally, even if they are verbally abusive. Instead, I sympathize with their position and assure them that we’ll come up with an effective solution for them. I know how frustrating it can get when troubleshooting issues.

"I’m sorry to hear about this issue. If you will please answer a few questions for me, I’m sure that we can resolve it to your satisfaction."

If you didn't understand the customer or they were being rude, what would you do?
If I don't understand what they have said and just need some clarification, I will respectfully ask them to repeat themselves, maybe they need to speak slower or more clearly. Involving them in the troubleshooting process is a good practice. I would ask for a detailed description of the problem they are having and what steps they have taken to resolve the issue so far. Walk them through new solutions to try and hopefully resolving their issue without any further escalation.

If you are not sure what a coworker meant, what would you do?
Ask them to explain what they meant or their intentions in greater detail.
